Global SIM Card Solution

Introduction​

AutoPi offers a global SIM card solution. The SIM card solution is mainly targeted for our CAN-FD Pro and TMU CM4 (without simcard) solutions, as these does not come with a SIM card per standard. The solution can be purchased in our webshop. We offer different package sizes and your monthly data usage is shown from our Cloud solution.

tip

In case you have any questions, don't hesitate to contact us at support@autopi.io.
